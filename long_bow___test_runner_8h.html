Allocate and initialise a LongBowTestRunner structure with the given parameters.

See longBowTestRunner_Destroy to destroy the result.

The parameters may be static values (ie. not allocated) supplied by the caller and they are not subject to free in longBowTestRunner_Destroy.

Every LongBow test case has an associated "clipboard" that is specific to a test case and is shared between the fixture setup and tear down functions. Fixture setup may store things on the clipboard, the test case may access them and the tear down destroy them.
